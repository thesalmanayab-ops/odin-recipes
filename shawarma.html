Shawarma

Description
This chicken shawarma is made with flavorful chicken thighs marinated in a spiced yogurt.
Slice the cooked chicken and serve in pita bread with tomato, sliced onion,
and my homemade tahini sauce for a popular Middle Eastern sandwich. Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Additional Time: 4 hrs
Total Time: 4 hrs 50 mins
Servings: 8

    Ingredients

  • ½ cup malt vinegar
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon mixed spice
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground cardamom
  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup tahini
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • ½ teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 medium tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup sliced onion
  • 4 cups shredded lettuce
  • 8 pita bread rounds

    Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
    Ingredients for chicken shawarma, including chicken, pita bread, lettuce,
    tomato, yogurt, spices, and sauces, arranged on a surface
  2. To make the marinade: Combine malt vinegar, 1/4 cup yogurt, vegetable oil, mixed spice,
    cardamom, salt, and pepper together in a glass baking dish.
    Marinade for chicken shawarma in a clear dish
  3. Add chicken thighs to the mixture and turn to coat.
    Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
    Chicken thighs marinating in a glass dish
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    To make the tahini sauce: Combine tahini, 1/4 cup yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil,
    and parsley together in a small bowl.
    Season with salt and pepper, taste, and adjust flavors if desired.
    Cover and refrigerate.
    A bowl of creamy shawarma sauce with herbs, placed on a light surface
  5. Cover the chicken and bake in the marinade for 30 minutes, turning once.
    Uncover, and cook for an additional 5 to 10 minutes, or until chicken is browned and cooked through.
    Glass baking dish with cooked chicken pieces in juices
  6. Remove chicken from the dish, and cut into slices.
    Sliced cooked chicken on a cutting board, with a baking dish nearby
  7. Place sliced chicken, tomato, onion, and lettuce onto pita breads.
    Roll up, and top with tahini sauce.
    Four chicken shawarma wraps with vegetables and sauce
